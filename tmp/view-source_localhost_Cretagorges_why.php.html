Why Book a walk with Creta Hikes...
Home > Why Walk With Us
 

Our mission is to introduce you to hiking in the stunning landscapes of Creta's Island.

 

New friendships

Take a group of people from diverse areas of the world, most not knowing each other. Put them together on a walk through some of the most isolated areas of Crete and discover the amazing friendships which develop as a result!
 

Creta's finest guided walking company

Creta's finest guided walking company Extensive time, effort and investment has gone into upgrading our services and facilities over the years. As a result, Creta Hikes is a world-class trekking venture with an international reputation, attracting thousands of walkers a year. We offer a range of multiday and single day walks that promise to be the experience of a lifetime, where you will truly encounter the best of Creta's wilderness without sacrificing comfort or convenience. Our walks are all inclusive and fully catered. Our guides are fully trained in first aid and are in radio contact with each other and our base at all times. On overnight walks accommodation is in our private, custom-built lodges. With hot showers, drying rooms, bedding and all meals - what more could you want

You're in good hands

Our guides are trained outdoor enthusiasts who will explain all there is to know about this amazing region. They pride themselves on their safe, efficient and friendly service and will be with you as much, or as little as you need.

 

Receive 10% off your next walk

We know that once you have experienced one of our walks you'll be hooked!

So if you've done a walk with us and then book another walk within 5 years, we'll give you 10% off your second walk - and any other walks you do in the future.

Simply tick 'Yes, I am an Creta Hikes Repeat Walker' as you complete the booking process. We'll verify your booking before we process your payment and send you confirmation.

Terms and Conditions

  1. We must be able to verify your previous booking before we can apply the 10% discount to your current booking.
  2. Your previous walk must have been within 5 years of the date you make your next booking.
  3. If your booking includes walkers who have not walked with Creta Hikes before, the discount will only be applicable to persons who have walked with Creta Hikes before.
